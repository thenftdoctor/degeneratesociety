Degenerate society

Degenerate society is a series of 1/1 NFT's by artist KDKammeijer with a truly unique style on the solana blockchain. There are two different NFT collections: Grand Masterpieces and Avatar Gods. Grand Masterpieces are large complex artworks depicting the chaos in our Degenerate Society. They are the main artworks of Degenerate Society and only 25 will be made. Avatar Gods are smaller artworks highlighting a character of the degenerate society, that are also well-suited to be used as PFP. The artworks have a black&white base with striking color accents, characteristic of KDKammeijer's style.

Karel D. Kammeijer

Karel D. Kammeijer is an Amsterdam-based artist who's been drawing and creating art since he could hold a pencil. His art and illustrations depict his imagination and the world around him in a complex and detailed way the eye can't get enough off.
After getting his bachelor degree in Fine Arts from the "Willem de Kooning Academie" in Rotterdam, he started specializing in making big detailed murals and paintings.
Now KDKammeijer is excited to join the world of NFT's and launches his first 1/1 collection "Degenerate Society" in collaboration with The NFT doctor.
Check out previous work by KDKammeijer on his personal website and instagram.

How to buy

Primary NFT sales:
Auctions are currently held on these exchange.art collection pages: Grand Masterpieces and Avatar Gods. Only Grand Masterpieces #1-3 were previously minted on holaplex. Follow KDKammeijer on twitter and join discord for notifications of new auctions.

Secondary market NFT sales:
After auctions, NFTs will be listed on secondary market at the exchange.art platform. Use the links under primary NFT sales to hunt for secondary listings.

If you are new to NFT's here are the steps you need to take:
1. Acquire enough SOL (solana's coin) through a centralized exchange like Coinbase, FTX, Binance or other.
2. Create a self-custodial solana wallet such as Phantom or Solflare. Write down your seed phrase (not on pc/smartphone/tablet) and safekeep it. Copy your public wallet address.
3. Send sufficient SOL from your centralized exchange to your self-custodial wallet address.
4. Go to an exchange.art collection page (Grand Masterpieces or Avatar Gods) connect your wallet (top right corner) and place a bid or buy listed NFT's.
