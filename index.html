How to buy:

Primary NFT sales will be through a auctions on KDKammeijer's Holaplex page.
Follow KDKammeijer on twitter for notifications of new auctions.
NFT's that have been sold via auctions will be listed on secondary market at the exchange.art platform.
Check the exchange.art collection page to hunt for secondary sales.

If you are new to NFT's here are the steps you need to take:

1. Acquire enough SOL (solana's coin) through a centralized exchange like Coinbase, FTX, Binance or other.
2. Create a self-custodial solana wallet such as Phantom or Solflare. Write down your seed phrase (not on pc/smartphone/tablet) and safekeep it. Copy your wallet address.
3. Send sufficient SOL from your centralized exchange to your self-custodial wallet address.
4. Go to KDKammeijer's Holaplex collection page connect your wallet (top right corner) and place a bid, or go to the exchange art page to buy on secondary market.
